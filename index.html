The problem

Tax time should not feel like this.

Glove boxes full of fading petrol receipts. The accountant’s invoice climbing every year. BAS overdue. Subscriptions you forgot you had. We have spoken to enough Australians to know none of it is working as it should.

Lost receipts, lost money

Most Australians lose at least one receipt that would have been deductible every year. Faded petrol slips. Bunnings dockets that went through the wash. Email confirmations buried in three different inboxes.

The annual scramble

30 June arrives. The shoebox comes out. Three weekends disappear into manual receipt sorting before you even get to your accountant. Most people leave deductions on the table just to be done with it.

Data scattered everywhere

Bank statements in one app. Receipts in a folder somewhere. Subscription invoices in email. BAS records in Xero. None of it joined up. Tax time becomes an archaeology project.

You should not need a spreadsheet

The tools that exist either ask you to photograph every receipt or to learn an accounting system. Most people give up. They want the platform to do the work. We agree.

FAQ

The honest answers.

Why are you asking for survey responses instead of just launching?

Because we are not done building yet. The survey takes 2 minutes and the answers shape what we prioritise. Sole traders get one set of features ranked first. PAYG taxpayers another. We would rather hear from you now than guess and rebuild later.

Does Finwell AI give me financial advice?

No. We organise your spending and report it back. We will tell you what you bought, when, how often, and how it is categorised. We will not tell you what to buy, what to cut, or what to invest in. Decisions stay with you. For advice on your circumstances, see a registered tax agent or licensed financial adviser.

Does it replace my accountant?

No. It makes them faster and cheaper. You hand them a clean, pre-categorised dataset instead of a shoebox. Your accountant gets desktop access to fine-tune categories and prepare your lodgement. They focus on judgement calls, not data entry.

Is it safe? Is my financial data protected?

Yes, and this is not marketing speak. Account setup runs through licensed Australian infrastructure. We never see card numbers. We cannot move money. We receive transaction data only, encrypted in transit and at rest. You can disconnect anytime. You can export or delete everything you have given us, anytime.

What does “item-level” mean?

Bank apps see “Coles, $87.” Finwell AI sees the milk, the bread, the dog food, the chocolate bar that made up that $87. The difference is whether you actually know where the money goes, or just guess.

I do not pay much tax. Is this still for me?

Yes. Plenty of people use Finwell AI just as a budget tool. You get taxable item breakdowns, the AI search bar to ask anything, and one clean record across everything you spend. The day your tax position changes, your history is already there.

When does it actually launch?

Public launch in 2026. Founding members who take the survey get early beta access before then. Take the survey, you are in line.

Is this just for big-city types?

No. Sparky in Geelong, teacher in Cairns, side hustle in Hobart, fishing charter out of Port Douglas. Same platform, same way. Built for any Australian who has to deal with tax, BAS, or just keeping track of where the money goes.
